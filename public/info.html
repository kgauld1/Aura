Welcome to Aura!





Aura is your personal musical companion designed to monitor your emotions and generate music to fit your mood. Turn on your webcam and press play to hear music generated by your very own emotions! Using your webcam, Aura detects your mood through your facial expression, categorizing it as happy, neutral, or sad. Then, Aura uses machine learning to generate music reflective of your current emotional state. Aura can also be customized to your preferences to give you the best experience possible. You can customize your listening by selecting to include drums and nature sounds in addition to the instrumentals.


Not interested in using the webcam? No worries! Turn off your camera and selected your mood with the skip and previous buttons underneath the emoji.



Wondering which emotions you've expressed? Check out 'Analytics' to view how long you've expressed each emotion.



Ready to get lost in the music? Click 'Home' to start listening and watch as Aura's animated gradient background changes colors with your mood.
